Nina Osborne
2nd year student at the University of Iowa

Hi! My name is Nina Osborne and I am a double-major in Geographical and Sustainability Science (GIScience track) and Environmental Policy, with a certificate in Applied Climate and Energy Technology. I am interested in learning any and all geospatial tools to make decisions about Earth. I do research under Dr. Susan Meerdink, evaluating spectral responses to changes in Bur Oak sapling environments. I also integrate sensors onto our DJI Matrice 300 drone. I learned HTML for my Introduction to Computer Science class in the Fall of 2024, so I am excited to make this portfolio. My first website was a green background, with one image of a toad. I don't currently have a particular career in mind, but I really like space and technology; I am excited to take Process and Design for Satellites next semester. Go Hawks!
